Bubble Gaga

Coco Tea Shop

4

I tried this after being stuffed stomach-full with sushi and it still blew my mind – and taste buds – away. An atypical pairing between your typical milk tea (which, certainly well made, but is also not over-sugared) with the sweet creamy torched crème brûlée you find off the streets of Paris and London (a connoisseur must know the origins of deserts). A specialty drink and a must-try if you visit Machi Machi; albeit a little bit pricey, it'll still be well worth it!
